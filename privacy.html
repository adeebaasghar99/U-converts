Privacy Policy

Your Trust Matters to Us

Why Your Privacy Matters

We’re more than just a fast and accurate unit conversion tool—we’re a purpose-driven platform supporting orphans globally. We respect your privacy, never sell your data, and keep your experience ad-free and secure.

What Information We Collect

Uconverts.com is a no-signup, no-login service. We collect limited non-personal data to improve performance and user experience, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Anonymous IP address
  • Language preference
  • Usage patterns (most used unit converters)

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information.

Use of Cookies

We use only essential cookies to enhance your experience. These help us:

  • Remember your preferred settings (e.g., last used unit type)
  • Track anonymous site usage via tools like Google Analytics
  • Improve speed and reliability of our converters

No third-party ad cookies. No invasive tracking. No data sales. Ever.

Third-Party Tools

We may use privacy-respecting tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Cloudflare) for security and site optimization. These services comply with GDPR, CCPA, and industry best practices.

Your Data Is Never Sold

We do not display ads, and we never sell or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

Our Motivation and Purpose

Your visits help grow a platform that supports global orphan care, education, and well-being. By simply using our converter, you’re contributing to a greater cause.

Your Rights & Control

You can manage cookies via your browser or use incognito mode for an extra layer of privacy. Under GDPR/CCPA laws (if applicable), you have the right to:

  • Access any stored data
  • Request deletion of anonymized usage data
  • Know how your data is handled

We make it easy—just email us at uconvertss@gmail.com.

Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. Changes will be posted with a new effective date at the top.
